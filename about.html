Hi! I’m Septiana, but you can call me Tia. I’m a cheerful student with big dreams and a soft spot for laying in bed while endlessly scrolling through TikTok. That’s my kind of healing, don’t judge!

Food is my love language. I’m obsessed with spicy crispy chicken (especially with melted cheese), chocolate, ice cream, and of course—Cimory milk, all flavors welcome. If my fridge is full of those, life is good.

I’m not a super serious person, but I know what I want. Life’s too short to be gloomy, so I live it with pastel colors, happy vibes, and lots of silly moments. This website? Just a cute little version of me—sweet, quirky, and full of personality.
